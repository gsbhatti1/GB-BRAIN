> Strategy Arguments



|Argument|Default|Description|
|----|----|----|
|N|0.002|Fund utilization rate or lot size|
|XX|true|Double value|
|sssss|true|Real offer parameter coefficient 2|
|TC|0.001|First order quantity|
|FB|4|Maximum doubling times|
|CJ|2|Location coefficient|
|BLD|false|1=Open a position on the middle track-0=Open a position on the upper or lower track|
|FX|2|0 long-1 short-2 double|
|len|26|trendline period|
|CJ2|2|Closing coefficient|
