## Summary

This strategy integrates the trend judgment of dual EMAs and the risk control of the ATR volatility indicator to form a relatively complete trend following system. The focus for optimization is adjusting the moving average and ATR parameters to match the product characteristics, and designing dynamic stop loss/take profit mechanisms to follow changes in trend strength. ## Conclusion

This strategy combines the trend determination using dual EMAs and the risk management using the ATR volatility indicator to create a relatively complete trend-following system. The key to optimization lies in adjusting the moving average and ATR parameters to better fit the characteristics of the underlying asset, and implementing dynamic stop loss and take profit mechanisms to respond to changes in trend strength. By optimizing both parameters and logic, this strategy can be refined into a robust trend-following strategy.
