> Strategy Arguments



|Argument Name| Default Value| Description|
|---|---|---|
|dema_length| 21| The length of the DEMA moving average|
|macd_fast| 12| The fast period of the MACD indicator|
|macd_slow| 26| The slow period of the MACD indicator|
|macd_signal| 9| The signal period of the MACD indicator|
|macd_hist确认| True| Whether to enable MACD histogram greater than 0 confirmation for buy signals|
|stop_loss| 0.01| The percentage stop loss level|
|take_profit| 0.02| The percentage take profit level|
|plot_dema| True| Whether to plot the DEMA line|
|plot_macd| True| Whether to plot the MACD histogram|
|plot_signals| True| Whether to plot buy and sell signals|
